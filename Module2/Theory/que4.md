4.  What SEO? 

EO stands for Search Engine Optimization.

It is the process of improving a website so that it ranks higher in search engines (like Google, Bing, Yahoo) and gets more visitors.

 In simple words:
SEO is like making your website easier for Google to find, understand, and recommend to users.

 How SEO Works :

When you search on Google, it shows results from millions of websites. SEO helps your site appear closer to the top by following certain techniques.

Search engines look at things like:

Keywords → Do the words people search for appear in your page content?

Content Quality → Is your content useful, original, and valuable?

Backlinks → Do other websites link to your site (trust factor)?

Website Speed → Is your site fast to load?

Mobile-Friendly → Does it work well on smartphones?

User Experience (UX) → Is it easy to read, navigate, and use?

Types of SEO :

On-Page SEO (things on your website)

Using keywords in titles, headings, and content.

Adding meta descriptions, alt text for images.

Making site easy to read and navigate.

Off-Page SEO (things outside your website)

Getting backlinks (links from other websites).

Social media sharing and mentions.

Building authority & trust.

Technical SEO

Improving site speed.

Mobile optimization.

Fixing broken links, sitemaps, indexing issues.

In Short  :

SEO = Making your website Google-friendly.

If done right, your site will show up higher in search results.

Higher ranking = More visibility → More visitors → More growth
